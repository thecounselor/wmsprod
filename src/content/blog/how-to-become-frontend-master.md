Becoming a frontend master is about more than just knowing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s about building beautiful, accessible, and high-performance web experiences that delight users. Here’s a roadmap to help you level up your frontend skills.

## 1. Master the Fundamentals

- **HTML:** Semantic markup, accessibility, SEO basics.
- **CSS:** Layout (Flexbox, Grid), responsive design, animations, preprocessors (Sass, Less).
- **JavaScript:** ES6+, DOM manipulation, event handling, fetch API, async/await.

## 2. Learn a Modern Framework

- **React, Vue, Svelte, or Astro:** Pick one and build real projects.
- Understand component-based architecture, state management, and routing.

## 3. Version Control & Tooling

- **Git:** Branching, merging, pull requests.
- **Build Tools:** Webpack, Vite, or Parcel.
- **Package Managers:** npm, pnpm, or yarn.

## 4. Testing & Debugging

- **Browser DevTools:** Inspect, debug, and profile your code.
- **Testing:** Unit and integration tests with Jest, Vitest, or Testing Library.

## 5. Performance & Accessibility

- Optimize images, lazy load assets, and minimize bundle size.
- Ensure your sites are accessible to all users (a11y best practices).

## 6. Build a Portfolio

- Create a personal website to showcase your projects.
- Contribute to open source or write technical articles.

## 7. Stay Curious

- Follow industry blogs, attend meetups, and keep experimenting with new tools.

## Conclusion

Frontend mastery is a journey, not a destination. Keep building, keep learning, and you’ll stand out as a true frontend expert.
